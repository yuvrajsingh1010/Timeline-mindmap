The manufacturing phase transforms design specifications and prototypes into mass-produced vehicles using efficient and cost-effective production methods. It involves sourcing raw materials, tooling, fabrication, assembly, quality control, and supply chain management. These activities are orchestrated to ensure timely delivery of high-quality products within budget constraints. By optimizing processes and managing resources effectively, manufacturers streamline production while maintaining product integrity. The manufacturing phase is critical for scaling up production and meeting market demand while upholding quality standards.
